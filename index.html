The Problem

Sustainability shouldn't need a research team.

Small business owners want to make responsible purchasing decisions. The tools to do it are either too expensive, too time-consuming, or simply don't exist at an accessible level.

"Speed and ease are the key to getting businesses to actually use the product."

— September Mills, Dive Shop Owner · Early Prototype Tester

Too Time-Consuming

Independent research takes hours or days per purchasing decision — simply not viable for everyday buying.

ERP Systems Are Too Costly

Enterprise sustainability tools are built for corporations. Small businesses simply can't afford the setup or licensing fees.

Incomplete Data

Self-built scorecards miss factory emissions and transport carbon — you only see what's already inside your own data.

How It Works

Works where you already buy.

EcoScore lives inside your browser — no new software, no learning curve.

01

Install the extension

Add EcoScore to Chrome or Firefox in seconds. No account required for the free tier.

02

Browse your suppliers

Visit any supplier or purchasing platform you already use. EcoScore automatically detects the company and pulls their sustainability data.

03

See scores. Decide better.

A clear sustainability score appears instantly — powered by government and community APIs. Premium users also see greener alternatives and transport carbon data.
